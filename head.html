Tiger

The Majestic King of the Jungle

About Tigers

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world. They are known for their powerful build, striking orange coat with black stripes, and their role as apex predators in the ecosystem.

Fun Facts

  • Tigers can weigh up to 300 kg (660 lbs).
  • They are excellent swimmers and enjoy water.
  • Each tiger has a unique stripe pattern, just like human fingerprints.
